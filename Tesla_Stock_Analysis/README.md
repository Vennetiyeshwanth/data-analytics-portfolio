## Tesla Stock Data Analysis (2010–2025)

### Dataset
- Historical Tesla stock data (2010–2025)

### Objectives
- Analyze yearly price trends
- Identify highest & lowest closing prices
- Compare volume traded over time

### Tools Used
- Google Sheets
- Pivot Tables
- Slicers
- Charts & Dashboard

### Key Insights
- Strong growth after 2020
- High volatility during major events
- Volume spikes during earnings years
